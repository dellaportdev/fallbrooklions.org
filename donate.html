Sight Campaign

Helping Students See to Succeed

Clear vision can be life changing. Your donation helps provide eye exams, eyeglasses, and essential vision services for local students who need support to succeed in school.

How You Can Help

Support a local child’s vision care

You can help a local child see more clearly and reach their full potential. Any amount you can give helps.

$25

Can help offset the cost of a vision screening.

$50

Can contribute toward an eye exam.

$100

Can help provide a complete exam and a pair of glasses.

Give through our Sight Campaign page

Donations made through the Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club Foundation support local student vision care through the Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club.

Eyeglass Donations

Donate your used eyeglasses

Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club can take your used eyeglasses. Donated glasses are sorted, refurbished, and given free to patients who receive vision screenings.

Drop them off at any of our donation boxes:

Major Market

845 S. Main Ave. Fallbrook

Community Center

341 Heald Ln. Fallbrook

Health & Wellness Center

1636 E. Mission Rd. Fallbrook

Tax-deductible donation note

Tax-deductible donations may be made to the Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club Foundation 501(c)(3). Donations to Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club are not tax-deductible. All donations to the foundation benefit Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club.

The Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club Foundation (EIN 41-4225752) is a tax-exempt organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. Contributions to the Foundation are tax-deductible as permitted by law. Contributions made directly to Fallbrook Ranch Lions Club are not tax-deductible.
